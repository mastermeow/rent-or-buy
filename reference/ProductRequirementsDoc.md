#### Product Requirements Document: Rent vs Buy Calculator

## 1. Product Purpose
This is a web application designed to help users intuitively compare the future financial impact of buying a home (with a loan) versus renting a home and investing in stocks, based on current market data and historical growth rates. It simulates calculations using user-defined input and built-in default data, providing numerical and graphical output to help users make more rational asset allocation decisions.

## 2. Core Functionalities

### 1. Hero Section
- Title: "Should I rent or buy a house?"
- Call-to-action buttons: "Edit My Inputs", "Share This Result"
- Calculation results
- Data source


### 2. Input Section
1. **Buy Parameters**
   - Basic data for buying a house:
     - Home Purchase Price (Default: $2000000)
     - Down Payment Ratio (Default: 20%) | Down Payment Amount 
     - Mortgage Interest Rate (Default: 6.75%)
     - Mortgage Term (Default: 30 years)
     
   - One-time transaction cost when buying a house:
     - Closing Cost (Default: 5% of home purchase price)

   - Recurring cost:
     - Property Tax Rate (Default: 1.25%/year)
     - Home Insurance and Maintenance (Default: 1% of home price/year)
     - HOA fees (Default: $0/month)

   - Value-added assumptions:
     - Home Appreciation | Compound Annual Growth Rate (Default: 4%/year)

   - Tax Impact:
     - Marginal Income Tax Rate (Default: 24%)
     - Property Tax Deductibility (Default: Yes)
     - Mortgage Interest Deductibility (Default: Yes)

   One-time transaction cost when selling a house:
     - Selling Cost (Default: 6% of home sale price)

2. **Rent and Invest Parameters**
   - Input fields for rental-related costs
     - Monthly rent
     - Renter's insurance
     - Security deposit
     - Annual rent increases
